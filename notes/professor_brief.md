Title: Fake news detection vs verification a comparative NLP study
Description : fake news research include both detection (classification of content) and verification (checking factual correctness) . This project compares these two approaches from NLP perspective .

	Student will analyze the strengths and limitations of detection-based methods vs simple verification pipelines based on textual evidence.

Core task: implement fake news detection models, explore claim verification using textual similarity or entailment, compare performance and error characteristics

Possible datasets:
Political and general fake news datasets
Claim verification datasets(English)

Future scope :
Explainable misinformation detection
Multi-step verification systems
